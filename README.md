### KPI Layer

The KPI layer provides aggregated business metrics used by the Revenue Intelligence Agent.

#### Views

* **vw_daily_revenue** – Daily revenue trend for anomaly detection.
* **vw_monthly_revenue** – Monthly revenue performance tracking.
* **vw_revenue_by_country** – Revenue contribution by country.
* **vw_revenue_by_category** – Revenue contribution by product category.
* **vw_revenue_by_product** – Product-level revenue analysis.
* **vw_revenue_by_customer** – Customer-level revenue contribution.

These views serve as the foundation for anomaly detection, root-cause analysis, forecasting, and AI-generated business insights.
